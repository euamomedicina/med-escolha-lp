# Image Generation Prompt Modifier — Med Escolha

This 60-word paragraph is prepended to every ad prompt so outputs match the brand DNA.

---

**MODIFIER (use as prepended block):**

Brand: Med Escolha — orientation product for medical specialty choice, sub-brand of Amo Medicina. Visual identity: deep navy (#0E1F4D) and saturated teal (#1FBFA8) as primaries, with white and warm yellow (#F4D14E) accents. Typography: modern geometric sans-serif (Poppins/DM Sans style), always lowercase headlines, key words highlighted with teal underline or background. Photography: soft natural lighting, warm color grading, smiling young doctors and medical students in white coats with stethoscopes, aspirational but accessible mood, never sterile or corporate-stiff. Layouts are spacious with generous whitespace. All text in Brazilian Portuguese.

---

## Typography rules for ad text

- All headlines and body copy: **lowercase only** (signature of the brand)
- Key 1-3 words per headline: **highlighted in teal** (#1FBFA8) — either underlined, color-changed, or with a translucent teal background bar
- CTA buttons: solid teal background, white lowercase text, medium-rounded rectangle (not pill)
- Standard CTA copy: "quero conhecer o med escolha"

## Logo treatment

- Wordmark is "med escolha" in lowercase black bold with a connector icon between "med" and "escolha" composed of TWO overlapping speech bubbles (red bubble on top, blue bubble below with three small white dots inside).
- Optional subtitle "por amo medicina" sits below "escolha" in smaller lowercase.
- Logo always placed in corner (top-left or bottom-right of the canvas), never centered or oversized.

## Color application rules

- **Navy** = high-impact statement frames, headline backgrounds, manifesto cards
- **Teal** = CTA, highlights, "muito mais do que um teste" call-outs, feature icons
- **Yellow** = "amo medicina" / pedigree mentions only — never as main field
- **White** = primary editorial background
- **Light gray (#F5F7F9)** = secondary card backgrounds

## Mood checklist (must-have on photo shots)

- Subject = medical student OR young/mid-career doctor
- Expression = calm confidence, soft smile, or contemplative gaze off-camera. NEVER stiff institutional pose, NEVER overly serious.
- Wardrobe = white coat, optional stethoscope, optional clipboard
- Lighting = soft natural, no harsh shadows
- Background = clean clinical OR warm modern interior, never sterile/cold
